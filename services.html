Our Best Services

Exchange

We collect various returned, damaged, faulty products delivered by our e-commerce clients and process and remarket them to maximize the utilization of that product.

Recycling

We sort, dismantle and segregate various core materials and components from the collected e-waste to reconfigure as raw materials to be used again

Last Mile Collection

We ensure last mile connectivity between individuals and organisations wanting to dispose e-waste and recyclers authorised by the state government.

Data Destruction

A key concern when sending your devices for e-waste management is the security of the confidential data stored in the hard drives of your PCs, laptops and servers. To prevent any breach of information, we provide an on-site 3X data destruction service at your doorstep.
